WE'RE SERIOUS ABOUT WEBSITES THAT LOOK STUNNING AND SIMPLY WORK

Say hello to Coastal – a modern and minimalist WordPress theme for today’s creative professional. Crafted for designers, illustrators, makers, artisans and photographers, Coastal is made for anyone with stunning work and a story to share.

We’ve made the design decisions so don’t have to; thoughtful typography, ample white space and just the right amount of color. You won’t find an endless list of features or unlimited layouts here, but if purposeful minimalism and monochrome simplicity get you excited, you’ve come to the right place.
